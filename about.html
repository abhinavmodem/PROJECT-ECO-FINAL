About Us

We are a dedicated team of friends passionate about waste management and environmental sustainability. Our mission is to make a positive impact on the environment by promoting responsible waste disposal, recycling, and education.

At Team Green Earth, we believe that everyone has a role to play in preserving our planet for future generations. We aim to raise awareness about the importance of waste management and empower individuals and communities to adopt sustainable practices.
